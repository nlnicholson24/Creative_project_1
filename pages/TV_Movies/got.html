TV & Movies by Lowell Nicholson

The marvelous creature pictured above is Bobby B. He was the star of Lowell's first and only attempt at a TV show, Game of Thrones. Unfortunately, Game of Thrones also happened to be the name of a book by much more successful author George R. R. Martin, and also happened to be in the process of having a TV show adaptation. So unfortunately, Lowell's creation never took off.

Game of Thrones is about Bobby B, the man who knows no limit when it comes to his mega belly. Bobby B is the freaking awesome king of a kingdom called Easteros, and uses his god-like laser powers to yell at and intimidate his inferiors. Of course, Bobby B's inferiors include everyone, so Bobby B just did a lot of laser eyes. There was no conflict in the story; Bobby B just laser-eyed everyone into doing whatever he wanted. Every enemy he ever had was crushed by the dopeness of his laser eyes combined with his beer belly. Long live Bobby B.
